About Us

CD BioSciences is a biological high-tech enterprise focusing on agricultural sciences, integrating R&D, production and application. It is mainly engaged in the related work of agricultural molecular design and breeding, and has rich experience in genetic marker development, genotyping detection, high-throughput sequencing-assisted group breeding, bioinformatics analysis, and molecular biology-assisted breeding. CD BioSciences provides a one-stop comprehensive service platform, and continuously improves service quality to meet customer needs. With the increasing recognition of our products and related services by customers, CD BioSciences has become a world-renowned biological brand.

Our Mission

Our Vision
